Privacy

Privacy.

How Aximo collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you contact us, apply to join, or engage one of our expert partners.

Last updated: this version is a working draft. The final policy will be in place at public launch.

What we collect

When you submit a form on this site, we collect your name, email, organisation, role, country, applicable interest, and a short description of what you are working on. We may also record the date and time of submission and an obscured reference to the page you applied from.

Why we collect it

We use the information to evaluate fit, respond to your enquiry, issue an invoice if you book a Diagnostic, and deliver the engagement you have requested. We do not sell or share your information with third parties for advertising.

Who sees it

Submitted information is reviewed by Aximo directors and, where matching is in progress, the relevant vetted expert partner. Anyone who sees your information is bound by a written confidentiality obligation through their Expert Partner Agreement.

Payments

No payment is collected on application. If your application is accepted, we send an invoice through a regulated banking or payment channel. We do not store card or account details on this site.

Email and lists

If you subscribe to The Aximo Brief, we add your email to a single newsletter list. You can unsubscribe with one click in any email. We do not use your address for any other purpose.

Cookies

This site uses only the cookies required for the page to function. We do not run third party advertising or behavioural tracking pixels.

Your rights

You may request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Send the request to hello@aximohub.com and we will respond within ten business days.

Retention

We hold enquiry records for as long as the conversation is active and for a reasonable period after, in line with Kenyan record keeping requirements. Client records are held for the duration of the engagement and for the legally required period after it ends.

Contact

For any privacy enquiry write to hello@aximohub.com with the subject line "Privacy".
