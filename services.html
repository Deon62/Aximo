Five disciplines
01

For compliance and contracts

Legal.

Entity setup, regulatory compliance, contracts, and ongoing legal support. Our expert partners work with foreign companies, diaspora founders, and Kenyan SMEs every week.

Typical engagements

  • Company incorporation and ownership structuring
  • Tax compliance set-up with KRA
  • Employment, supplier, and shareholder contracts
  • Sector regulatory navigation
02

For capital and structuring

Finance.

Tax structuring, banking, financial modelling, and capital advisory. Our partners understand how the Kenya Revenue Authority interprets, and how local banks actually decide.

Typical engagements

  • Banking and operational accounts set up cleanly
  • Tax structuring for cross-border revenue
  • Financial modelling and unit economics
  • Capital advisory for debt and equity
03

For positioning and distribution

Marketing.

Local positioning, distribution strategy, and brand work shaped around how Kenyan customers actually choose. Not how a brand book imagines they should.

Typical engagements

  • Localised brand narrative and value propositions
  • Pricing strategy and willingness to pay research
  • Distribution pipeline and channel selection
  • Launch and ongoing communications
04

For first time market presence

Market Entry.

Sector intelligence, partnership pipelines, and warm introductions. For companies entering Kenya for the first time, or relocating back home from diaspora.

Typical engagements

  • Sector deep dive and competitor mapping
  • Named partnership target list with warm introductions
  • Regulator engagement strategy
  • Go to market and first six months of presence
05

For systems and software

Technology.

If it runs on the internet, we can build it. Websites, web apps, system integrations, automations, and blockchain. From a one-page site to on-chain settlement, end to end.

Typical engagements

  • Websites, web apps, and product builds
  • System integrations and process automation
  • Blockchain and emerging tech work
  • Cloud, hosting, and technical infrastructure
